5. MATHEMATICS IMPROVEMENTS

A. Add calculators.
Add professional calculators with formulas and explanations.

For centrifuge:
- RCF from RPM and radius:
  RCF = 1.118 x 10^-5 x r x N^2
- RPM from RCF and radius:
  RPM = sqrt(RCF / (1.118 x 10^-5 x r))
- Rotor radius conversion: mm to cm.
- Tube balance validator.
- Run time estimator.
- Density gradient planner.

For AAS:
- Beer-Lambert law:
  A = epsilon x b x c
- Calibration curve slope and intercept.
- Unknown concentration from absorbance.
- Dilution factor:
  C1V1 = C2V2
- Limit of detection and limit of quantification.
- Percent error.
- Standard deviation.
- Relative standard deviation.
- Confidence interval for repeated measurements.

7. CLINICAL AND LABORATORY SAFETY ADVICE

This app should not present itself as a source of clinical diagnosis or medical decision-making. It should be positioned as an educational and laboratory training platform. If it is used in clinical laboratory education, add a clear disclaimer:

"This platform is for educational and training purposes only. It does not replace institutional SOPs, manufacturer instructions, regulatory requirements, or supervision by qualified laboratory personnel."

Add safety features:
- Pre-use safety checklist.
- PPE checklist.
- Emergency stop guide.
- Hazard symbols.
- Incident reporting template.
- Chemical spill response.
- Fire/gas leak response.
- Broken glass response.
- Biohazard response for biological samples.
- Electrical safety checklist.
- Centrifuge imbalance warning guide.
- AAS flame and gas safety guide.

For centrifuge:
- Always balance tubes.
- Never exceed rotor speed limits.
- Inspect tubes for cracks.
- Use compatible tubes.
- Do not open lid until rotor stops.
- Stop immediately if abnormal vibration or noise occurs.

For AAS:
- Check gas connections for leaks.
- Use proper ventilation.
- Follow flame ignition and shutdown procedures.
- Handle acid-digested samples carefully.
- Dispose of heavy metal waste properly.
- Do not operate without training and supervision.

13. FINAL PROFESSIONAL OPINION

Centraas has strong potential to become a serious laboratory education platform. The strongest foundation is the practical content on AAS and centrifuge machines. To make it professional, the next major step is not only adding more pages; it is adding structure: learning paths, progress tracking, safety checklists, calculations, simulations, references, and instructor tools.

As a software engineering project, it should become more maintainable by refactoring duplicated quiz logic and adding tests. As an educational platform, it should guide students step by step from theory to practical operation. As an engineering and chemistry learning tool, it should include calculations, troubleshooting, safety, maintenance, and quality control. As a clinical or laboratory training tool, it must clearly state that it supports education and training only; it must not replace official SOPs, manufacturer manuals, qualified supervision, or institutional safety rules.

If implemented properly, this app can become a professional digital laboratory trainer for students, instructors, and laboratory technologists.
